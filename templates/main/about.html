About Sakthi Groups

Since 1984, Sakthi Group has built a legacy of trust, quality, and disciplined execution. Through Sakthi Property, we extend this heritage into real estate developing thoughtfully crafted residential and commercial spaces. All our projects are RERA-approved, with select developments holding BDA approvals and E-Khata certification, ensuring transparency, legal clarity, and lasting value.

Who we are ?

Sakthi Property, a proud part of the renowned Sakthi Group of Companies, has been a trusted name in Chennai's real estate industry for over four decades. Our journey is built on a strong foundation of quality, integrity, and on-time delivery, creating architecturally elegant homes that blend seamlessly with their surroundings.

Our developments reflect a careful balance of thoughtful planning, superior construction standards, and enduring aesthetics. Each project is designed with a focus on natural light, efficient layouts, and long-term livability, ensuring spaces that remain relevant and refined over time.

Driven by a transparent and customer-first approach, we have successfully delivered premium residential projects to over 5000+ satisfied customers. Every home we build is thoughtfully designed to suit modern lifestyles, offering comfort, durability, and long-term value.

Frequently Asked
Question

Here are some common questions along with their answers to help clear up any confusion.

How long does it take to complete a construction project?

The timeline depends on the size and complexity of the project. A standard residential home can take anywhere from 12 to 24 months, while commercial or custom projects may take longer. We provide a detailed schedule during planning to keep you informed every step of the way.

What is included in the construction cost?

Construction costs typically cover materials, labor, machinery, and permits. Optional costs may include interior finishes, landscaping, and custom features. We offer a transparent breakdown so you know exactly what you’re investing in.

Can I make changes during the construction process?

Yes! Minor changes can often be accommodated during construction. For major changes, we review the impact on the timeline and cost before implementation to ensure your project stays on track.

How do you ensure the quality and safety of the construction?

We follow strict quality control and safety protocols at every stage. All materials meet industry standards, skilled professionals manage the work, and regular inspections ensure that your project is safe, durable, and built to last.

How can I contact BuildingBlocks Real Estate?

You can contact us via phone, email, or through the contact form on our website.
